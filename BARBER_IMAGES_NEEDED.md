## Image Specifications

- **Format:** PNG (preferred) or high-quality JPG
- **Recommended Size:** 800x1000px (portrait orientation)
- **Aspect Ratio:** 4:5 or similar portrait ratio
- **Background:** Professional, clean background (solid or blurred)
- **Style:** Consistent professional headshots or upper-body shots

## How to Add Images

1. Save your barber photos with the exact filenames listed above
2. Place them in the `/public/barbers/` directory
3. The website will automatically display them
