1-  Cristian Perdomo - Master Barber and Educator

Chief Operating Officer (COO) of House Of Havana Barbershop

Cristian is a master barber and entrepreneur, originally from Havana, Cuba. He discovered his passion for barbering at the age of 13, which grew into a professional career after moving to Canada at 19. Starting in Saskatoon and later relocating to Vancouver, Cristian refined his skills under strong mentorship and built a high-profile clientele, including entrepreneurs, artists, and entertainers. After achieving success, he and his partner welcomed twin children and chose to return to Saskatoon to focus on family and community. Outside of work, Cristian enjoys hiking, golfing, reading, and spending time with his family, always striving for personal and professional growth.

2-  CJ Galaura – Professional Barber
CJ Galaura began his barbering journey in 2020 during the COVID-19 pandemic, sparked by a simple haircut for a family member using a basic home kit. What started as a hobby quickly evolved into a deep passion for the craft. Self-taught through countless hours of practice and study, CJ began cutting hair in unconventional places—from school bathrooms to sidelines at football games—building both skill and a loyal client base.

Through consistency and dedication, CJ turned a quarantine hobby into a thriving career. Today, his clientele ranges from everyday clients to professional athletes, including recent opportunities to cut for pro basketball players—milestones that reflect how far his journey has come.

For CJ, barbering is more than just delivering a clean cut; it’s about building connection, confidence, and creating a memorable experience with every visit to the chair.

3-  Kurt – Professional Barber
Originally from the Philippines, Kurt moved to Canada in 2010 and discovered his passion for barbering during high school, cutting friends’ hair as a hobby. That early interest soon turned into a full-fledged career, and with over three years of professional experience, barbering has become much more than a job—it’s a true calling.

Kurt thrives on the ever-evolving nature of the industry, staying up-to-date with the latest styles, techniques, and trends to ensure every client leaves the chair with a fresh, modern look. His approach goes beyond the haircut; it’s about creating a comfortable, welcoming experience where clients can relax, recharge, and walk away feeling confident.

Outside the barbershop, Kurt enjoys working out, exploring automotive projects, discovering new cuisines, listening to music, and spending time with loved ones. These interests fuel his creativity and reflect the same focus and passion he brings to every cut.

4-  Janna – Professional Barber & Stylist
Born and raised in Saskatoon, Janna began her career in hair after graduating from cosmetology school in 2017. With a strong foundation in both women’s hairstyling and men’s grooming—including bridal and graduation styles—she has developed a versatile and client-focused approach to her craft.

Early in her journey, Janna was guided by the values of passion and work ethic over competition, shaping her perspective on hairstyling as a form of expression and connection. Inspired by her aunt, a hairstylist in Calgary, Janna moved there to refine her skills and explore new opportunities before returning to Saskatoon during the pandemic.

Today, she works as a full-time barber while also contributing part-time in senior care. Known for her technical precision and warm, compassionate style, Janna blends creativity with professionalism to offer

every client a personalized and meaningful experience. Outside the barbershop, she enjoys cooking, social dancing, fitness, and spending time with family.

5-  Sarah Alphonse- Professional-Journeyman Barber

Sarah, a proud Denesuline woman, was born in Saskatoon and raised in northern Saskatchewan. She discovered barbering in 2016, starting out with the idea that it would be a simple hobby to fall back on. What began as a side interest quickly became a lifelong passion. Over the years, she has honed her craft in barbershops across the city, learning, growing, and developing her own style along the way.

Now a certified Journeyperson Barber, Sarah specializes in hot shaves, skin fades, and beard care, services she’s come to love for the precision, artistry, and confidence they bring to her clients. For her, barbering is more than grooming; it’s about creating an experience where clients feel comfortable, cared for, and leave the chair feeling their best. With a deep respect for her craft and the people who trust her with their hair, Sarah takes pride in the connections she builds and the community she serves.

When she’s not behind the chair, Sarah enjoys crocheting, playing guitar, diving into PC games and spending time with her friends and family.

6-Kyle – Barber | Former Touring Musician

Originally from Yorkton, Saskatchewan, Kyle made the bold leap from the stage to the salon, trading a decade-long career as a professional touring musician for the barber’s chair in 2019. Bringing the same energy and creativity that fueled his music career, Kyle quickly established himself as a standout talent in Calgary’s barbering scene.

After honing his skills in Calgary, he became a go-to barber for the Calgary Flames, earning a reputation for his precision, speed, and signature style. Kyle specializes in longer, expressive cuts—from mullets and hockey flows to shaggy mod styles and blowout tapers—while also delivering crisp skin fades with expert precision.

Now based in Saskatoon, Kyle continues to blend creativity with craftsmanship behind the chair, while staying active in the music scene as a writer and producer. When he’s not cutting hair or making music, he enjoys life with his partner, kids, and a spirited crew of cats.
